Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly to us. This includes account details such as your name, email address, and password (stored securely in hashed form), as well as the expense entries you log — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve the Service: to authenticate your account, display your expenses and summaries back to you, and help you understand your spending patterns. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers and protected by industry-standard safeguards. Passwords are never stored in plain text. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage on the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of trusted third-party providers for hosting, analytics, or error reporting. These providers only receive the minimum data needed to perform their function and are bound by their own privacy and security obligations. We do not share your individual expense entries with any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data associated with your account at any time. You can update your information from your profile, or request full deletion by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out through the contact channels listed on our site.
